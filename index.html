About Courtney Morrissey:


Courtney Morrissey is a self-proclaimed trail princess who works in GIS and web development. She grew up in Upstate New York, lived in Boston, and has now settled in Boulder, Colorado, where she no longer feels out of place with her wolf shirts and birken-socks.


Her passions include wolves, wolf shirts, skiing, paddleboarding, backpacking, and binge watching Friends on Netflix because she is too afraid to get sucked into a new show. She used to do GIS for the Colorado Division of Water Resources and the USGS, and is now studying web development at Galvanize to enahce her GIS developer career.


Courtney made this site to satisfy the demands of friends' constant requests for trail suggestions in the Denver and Boulder foothills. One of the most important features of this site is the capability to redirect hikers away from Chautauqua on weekends, which is a quick way to improve the foothills hiking experience.
