Hike Recommendations:

Foothills Hikes in the Denver/Boulder Area

I’ve put together a list of some of my favorite “go-to” hikes in the vicinity of Denver and Boulder. I’ve mostly done stuff in this part of town so unfortunately I don’t have much info on the trails near Littleton and to the south, even though there are many.

These hikes are organized by theme, per the types of questions people usually ask me when looking for recs. i.e., “What’s a hike that’s short but steep for a good workout when I don’t have much time,” or “where can I take my sister who’s visiting and wants something pretty but easy?”

  • Favorite for After Work - Golden Area
    Trail Name Location Miles (RT) Elev. Gain Trailhead Notes
    Mt. Galbraith Golden 4.2 1100' Off Golden Gate Canyon Rd Great, short summit hike with extensive views
    White Ranch - Belcher Hill/Whippletree Loop North Golden 3 500' Off W 56th Road in Golden Gentle trail with nice foothills shrubbery
    Mt. Falcon - Turkey Trot/Castle Loop Morrison 3.2 800' Off of Rt 8 just south of Red Rocks (not to be confused with West entrance on 285) Pleasant loop trail with option to continue to summit ridge with further mountain views
    Dakota Ridge Morrison 6 1460' Matthews/Winter Park Enjoyable trail that has the benefit of being especially close to Denver.
  • Favorite for An (Easier) Trail Run
    Trail Name Location Miles (RT) Elev. Gain Trailhead Notes
    Matthews/Winter Park Morrison 3 500' Off 93 just north of Red Rocks Rolling gradient, with views of Red Rocks
    North Table Mountain North Golden 5.5 1000' Trailhead off 93 just north of town Steep hike to top is great for warmups, then flat mesa top is perfect for running. Loop connects to trailhead.
    Flatirons Vista Arvada/Boulder 3.5 100' Flatirons Vista Trailhead Broad, flat trail great for running, with spectacular views of the Flatirons and options to link to further trails
    Trading Post Loop Red Rocks 1.4 200' Next to Trading Post building at Red Rocks Park Extremely gentle loop through gorgeous and impressive red rock formations.
    Dowdy Draw - Spring Brook Loop Eldorado Springs 5.2 700' Dowdy Draw Trailhead Gentle grade with great view of Flatirons. Be mindful of mountain bikers
  • Favorite for Sunrise in Boulder
    Trail Name Location Miles (RT) Elev. Gain Trailhead Notes
    Anemone Hill Mapleton Hill, Boulder 3 1040' Centennial Trailhead A beautiful trail that's a shorter alternative to Mt. Sanitas but will still provide a workout. Great views of both Continental Divide and Boulder Red Rocks.
    Dakota Ridge Mapleton Hill, Boulder 2.5 400' 4th St and Valley View Dr, Boulder Gentle trail up ridge that meets the Goat Trail on the notch that could continue up to the Sanitas summit. Full views of Boulder
    Woods Quarry South Boulder 2.6 Text Chautauqua Short hike to unique vantage point with benches made out of large rocks overlooking Boulder
    Flagstaff Mountain South Boulder 3 1000' Crown Rock on Flagstaff Road, after Panorama Point Flagstaff has many trails that all provide a great short hike, with many options to get to Flagstaff summit. This one departs from Crown Rock parking area
  • Favorite for a Good Workout in Boulder
    Trail Name Location Miles (RT) Elev. Gain Trailhead Notes
    Mt. Sanitas Mapleton Hill, Boulder 3.1 1350' Centennial Trailhead Avoid this on a weekend, but the classic Boulder summit loop that will provide great views and a great workout. Plenty of interesting rock formations along the ridge to the summit
    Flatiron 1/2 South Boulder 3 1400' Chautauqua A trail with a steady rise, often crossing talus along the Flatiron formation.
    Bear Peak South Boulder 8 3000' NCAR Great for 14er training. A longer day hike with an especially steep section in the middle leading up to a rocky summit.
    Royal Arch South Boulder 3.2 1400' Chautauqua Beautiful trail through scenic woods at Chautauqua Park that takes you to a unique geological formation.
    Gregory Canyon South Boulder 2.3 900' Gregory Canyon Trailhead Great, short but intense hike for a quick workout.
  • Favorite for a Saturday when Chautauqua is Crowded
    Trail Name Location Miles (RT) Elev. Gain Trailhead Notes
    Anemone Hill Mapleton Hill, Boulder 3 1040' Centennial Trailhead A beautiful trail that's a shorter alternative to Mt. Sanitas but will still provide a workout. Great views of both Continental Divide and Boulder Red Rocks.
    Tenderfoof Loop Flagstaff Mountain 2.2 400' Realization Point Trailhead Winding, gentle trail through woods with views of the Indian Peaks
    Mesa-Big Bluestem Loop Eldorado Springs 4.5 800' South Mesa Trailhead Unique, rolling wooded landscape against South Boulder Peak and Devil's Thumb
    Wapiti/Ponderosa Loop Trail Heil Valley Ranch 7.7 1000' Heil Valley Ranch Main Trailhead Gradual loop through meadows and woods.
